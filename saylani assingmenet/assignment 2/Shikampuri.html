Shikampuri kebab

Shikampuri kebab is a traditional meat dish originating from India. These meat patties are usually made from mutton or chicken mixed with ginger, garlic, eggs, chana dal (bengal gram), and spices such as cardamom, cinnamon, red chili powder, and turmeric powder.
